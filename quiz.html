We know that as an incoming freshman you may not know what you want to do as a major. If you are undecided or not sure if the major you chose is right for you, try taking some of these quizzes! These quizzes will suggest what your major should be based on your answers.

Villanova University also provides you with helpful resources on exploring majors.

Quizzes

Site Name Description
    ASA Futurescape
This webstie is designed to determine your future with an interactive quiz. Through their 11 question quiz, the site predicts your personality and recommends potential futures for you. Try this quiz if you want to know what field you want to go into in the future.
    Indigo Pathway
This one is your basic "What's My Major" quiz. Just 10 minutes and it suggests you highly accurate and highly currated matches based on your strengths, motivators, and behaviors.

Villanova Resources

Site Name Description
    Villanova Programs
Villanova's own web page to help you explore the many programs that they offer. If you know what school you want to go into, check out our own website tabs for more in depth information.
    Academic Advising
If you are undecided, there is a high chance that you are in the Liberal Arts and Science school. In that case, check out this Villanova website that has all the information on advisors. You are already matched with an advisor and you can find their contact information here as well. Advisors help you to pick classes and narrow down what your interests are, so take advantage of them!
    Career Center
This webpage from the Villanova website gives you lots of options to explore your future major. You can explore careers and even see the outcomes by major. Once you have a major, they can even help you get a job by working on your resume and cover letters.
They also suggest "Handshake" where you can make an appointment with an expert to discuss your plans or to explore your choices on a major.
