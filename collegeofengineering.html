College of Engineering

Villanova University

Engineering Majors Major Description
Chemical Engineering In the Chemical Engineering program, you will learn how to solve problems using mathematics, chemistry, physics, and engineering sciences.
Civil Engineering In the Civil Engineering program, you will learn how to build infrastructure that protects the enviorment and provides clean water.
Computer Engineering In the Computer Engineering program, you will learn how to develop and design computer systems by using computer sciences and electronic engineering.
Electrical Engineering In the Electrical Engineering program, you will learn about electricty, electronics, and electromagnetism. You will work with electrical systems and their components conducting research and design.
Mechanical Engineering In the Mechanical Engineering program, you will learn to apply the principles of solid mechanics, fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, heat transfer, material science, and dynamics and control. You will apply these principles by building machines and systems.
